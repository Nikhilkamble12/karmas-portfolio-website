Welcome to Karmas!

Founded in 2025 by Nikhil Kamble, Karmas is on a mission to solve some of India’s biggest challenges by harnessing the transformative power of technology. Through the Karmas application, we strive to address critical issues such as education, healthcare, agriculture, and women’s empowerment by partnering with NGOs and empowering individuals to create real change.

Features

  • Shayaka Feature

  • NGO Features

Shayaka Feature

  • Engage with the Community: The feed allows Shayaka to stay updated with real-time stories and initiatives happening around them.
  • Contribute Actively: With easy access to upload and add requests, Shayaka can contribute or seek help effortlessly.
  • Stay Connected: Through comments and interactions, users can connect with other Shayaka and Shayaka NGOs.
  • Track Impact: The Shayaka current score reflects their contributions, encouraging consistent participation.

NGO Features

  • Centralized Management Provides a single platform to manage requests, donations, and user interactions. New, Accepted, or Rejected.
  • Enhanced Visibility Showcases NGO activities and encourages user engagement through likes and support metrics.
  • Efficient Collaboration A facilitates a strong connection with the Karmas main platform, ensuring alignment with broader community goals.
  • Transparency Promotes accountability by providing clear statistics upon verified requests and confirmed donations .

Posts Feed

Displays posts from other Shayaka, Shayaka NGOs, or communities, including impactful stories, requests, or project updates.

Visual Media for Posts

Shayaka can upload and share images or videos, making it easier to showcase the real impact of contributions or highlight areas of need.

Scoreboard

A gamified feature that tracks Shayaka contributions with a "Current Score" indicator, thus motivating users to engage and contribute more to the community and earn rewards.

Verified Request Badge

Posts with blue checkmarks indicates authentic requests or contributors, building trust within the platform

Home

Shayaka to the dashboard to view posts, videos and their score.

Search

Allows Shayaka to find NGOs,, or other shayaka.

Add (+)

A quick-access button to submit new requests or contribute.

Notifications

Displays alerts for updates, new posts, or activity on the Shayaka contributions.

Profile

Sahayaks can view their followers List, following list, Score, and Requests which also they can edit at a time .
