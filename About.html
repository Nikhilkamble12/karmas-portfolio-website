Welcome to Karmas!

    Founded in 2025 by Nikhil Kamble, Karmas is on a mission to solve some of India’s biggest challenges by harnessing the transformative power of technology. Through the Karmas application, we strive to address critical issues such as education, healthcare, agriculture, and women’s empowerment by partnering with NGOs and empowering individuals to create real change. At Karmas, we believe that every individual has the potential to shape the future and make a difference. With this belief, we have created a platform that connects people with opportunities to contribute meaningfully to society, ensuring access to basic necessities for those in need.

How Karmas Work

    How Karmas Works Karmas is a social-impact platform designed to bridge the gap between those in need and the organizations or individuals willing to help. Users can post requests for assistance, whether for themselves, their community, or even animals, and Karmas ensures these requests reach NGOs or facilitators who can respond. Once a request is accepted and fulfilled, users earn Karma Points as recognition for their contributions. The platform also allows users to share their positive impact stories, engage with others through likes, comments, and shares, and build meaningful connections. Karmas empowers users to make a difference, track their impact, and create a collaborative community of change-makers.

Our Vision

    At Karmas, we believe in creating a world where kindness, compassion, and action bring people and communities together. Our platform bridges the gap between those in need and those who can help, including NGOs, volunteers, and everyday individuals. Through Karmas, users can share requests, offer support, and build meaningful connections, whether it's helping people, animals, or larger causes.
