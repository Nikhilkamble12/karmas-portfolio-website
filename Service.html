Service

Connecting users in need with NGOs through verified Karmas, ensuring genuine assistance and impactful support.

Key Services

NGO Partnerships

The Karmas app collaborates with verified NGOs specializing in various sectors such as education, healthcare, and women’s safety etc.

NGOs review requests and take action to provide timely and effective support.

Request System

Users can submit requests for assistance in education, healthcare, agriculture, or women’s empowerment etc.

Tracking & Transparency

Users can monitor the status of their requests in real-time through the app’s request tracking.

The platform ensures complete transparency by providing details about NGO involvement and progress updates.

Community Sharing

Beneficiaries and NGOs can share stories of success and impact, inspiring others to participate or seek help.

Posts with photos and videos create a sense of connection and trust among users.

Connecting Requests to Help

Users can post requests for assistance that are visible to NGOs.

NGOs review these requests and can choose to accept them

Once accepted, the requester earns points, promoting active participation.
